About Me

From Kathmandu
to Knox College.

I'm a Freshman at Knox College in Galesburg, Illinois, studying Data Science and Economics. Originally from Kathmandu, Nepal, I build at the intersection of machine learning, quantitative economics, and creative design.

Most recently I won the FinTech track at HackAugie 2026 with MindfulCart — a Chrome extension that adds a 30-second pause before online checkout. I've also built end-to-end ML pipelines trained on 1.3 million real loans with fairness audits and SHAP explainability.

Outside of code I've taught English and skating to students of all ages, worked as a barista at Himalayan Java Coffee in Kathmandu, and managed digital operations for a family business.
